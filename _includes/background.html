Background

I'm currently a Research Fellow with SCADR at Edinburgh Napier University, while finishing my PhD at University of Glasgow .

I have a background in psychology and sociology and hold an MRes in Research Methods and a MSc in Global Mental Health from University of Glasgow and a B.A.(Hons) in Psychology from University of the West of Scotland

As a social scientist interested in mental health, I want to bridge the gap between data analysis, ethics, and reflexivity while centering lived experiences — so knowledge can be used to inform policy, have a real-world impact, and improve lives.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I'm probably drumming, cycling with my dog, or knitting something impractical.
